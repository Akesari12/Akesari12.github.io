How is technology changing law and legal practice? This seminar explores the intersection of law and various forms of technology. This semester, we will place a strong focus on AI as it has increasingly come to dominate conversations around regulation, the economy, and the future of the legal profession.

Through a series of law review articles, blog posts, podcasts, and simulations, we will explore the basics of how technology works, and how it is changing law and legal practice. By the end of the course, students will be comfortable with consulting a wide variety of sources to keep up with fast-changing technologies like AI.
